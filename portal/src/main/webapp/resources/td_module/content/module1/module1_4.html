Why Getting it Right is Important
Why is it so important to make the right hire? How does a bad hire impact an organization?

Drag and drop the numbered circle displayed on each statement into the appropriate bucket to classify the impact of a good or a bad hire on an organization. Click Submit to view the feedback.

As an interviewer, bear in mind the impact of a bad hire on an organization and therefore, the need to hire the right person for any job. Inform candidates about what organizations look for while interviewing. Also explain their role in becoming a ‘good hire’, and the importance of having the right knowledge, skills and attitude towards the job they are applying for.

A good hire :

  • Contribute positively to the growth of the organization.
  • Make the organization not only bigger, but better.
  • Are an asset to the organization.

On the other hand, a bad hire:

  • Results in high rates of attrition.
  • Decreases the morale of other employees or the organization
  • Adds significantly to the recruiting and training costs.
  • Reduces revenue and productivity.
  • Has a ripple effect on various departments.
